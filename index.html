About Worldstar College

In year 2016, the administrators of Isabela Colleges of Science and Technology (est.1997) have decided to expand its horizons and go beyond offering computer courses. It also decided to operate under a name that is rooted from its previous identities. Now it is known as the Worldstar College of Science and Technology, Inc.. Up until today, the institution continues to provide prime quality of education and create competent individuals who are industry ready and contributes to the improvement and advancement of their fields. Throughout the years, ICST has been providing high quality yet affordable education, making it available to anyone who is willing to study and learn. They also offer several scholarships to students who are financially challenged but have the determination to finish schooling. ICST assures that each student gets equal opportunity to learn. Statistically speaking, 95% of the total graduates are now employed in different areas in the Philippines and some, overseas.
